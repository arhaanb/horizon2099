Cosmo is a multinational company specializing in the planning, development and distribution of video games and the research, development and distribution of software, data and video media. The company was founded in 2017 as a computer graphics producer. On January 16, 2018, the company launched a game development division and re-established itself as primarily a video game company. On September 30, 2018, Cosmo became a wholly owned subsidiary of MIS, in turn a subsidiary of MINET. Funded by more than 17 companies and partnered with 4 top bussinesses in the world right now, we help to create a long lasting human connection.
